# TrickyPro – Elite Fitness OS

A complete fitness tracking web app that works right in your browser. Track workouts, diet, progress, and streaks with a sleek cyberpunk interface.

## Features

### Dashboard
- View your current level, XP, and daily streak
- See key stats: weight, BMI, TDEE, protein goal
- Track weekly progress with visual rings
- Get today's workout at a glance
- Motivational fitness images

### Workout Plan
- Weekly workout schedule based on your goal (Lose/Gain/Maintain)
- Exercises with sets, reps, rest times, descriptions, and tips
- Mark exercises complete to earn XP
- Built-in rest timer for each exercise
- Exercises grouped by muscle: Chest, Back, Legs, Shoulders, Arms, Core, Cardio

### Diet & Nutrition
- Automatic calorie and macro calculation based on your profile
- Protein, Carbs, Fat, Fiber, and Water targets
- Full daily meal schedule with times and descriptions
- Hydration tracker - tap water glasses to log intake
- Supplement tracker - mark supplements as taken
- Food guide showing what to eat and avoid for your goal

### Progress Tracking
- Log your weight daily
- Interactive weight chart showing progress toward target
- BMI calculation and tracking
- Muscle coverage map showing which muscle groups you train most

### Streak System
- Current and best workout streaks
- Total workouts completed
- Weekly workout count
- Monthly calendar view showing workout days, rest days, and missed days
- Achievements unlocked by hitting milestones (streaks, total workouts, levels)

### Profile
- View and edit your biometrics (height, weight, age, gender)
- Change fitness goal and training level
- Adjust activity level for accurate calorie calculations
- Reset all data option

### AI Coach (Optional)
- Ask questions about diet, workouts, motivation, or recovery
- Quick buttons for common questions
- AI has context of your stats and progress

## How to Use

### First Time Setup
1. Open the app
2. Enter your name, height, weight, age, gender, activity level
3. Select your goal: Lose Fat, Build Muscle, or Maintain
4. Set your target weight and training level
5. Click LAUNCH to start

### Daily Routine
1. **Dashboard** - Check today's suggested workout
2. **Workout** - Complete exercises, use the rest timer, mark sets done
3. **Diet** - Log water, mark supplements taken, follow meal plan
4. **Progress** - Log your daily weight
5. **Streak** - Check your consistency and achievements

### Earn XP & Level Up
- Complete an exercise: +50 XP
- Finish all exercises in a day: +200 XP bonus
- Maintain streaks: bonus XP
- Level up increases every 200 XP

### Rest Timer
- Click the timer button on any exercise
- Default rest time from workout plan
- Add/subtract 15 seconds
- Pause/resume as needed

### AI Coach
- Click the robot icon at bottom left
- Type your question or use quick buttons
- Ask about nutrition, workout form, motivation, or progress

## Navigation

**Desktop** - Top navigation bar
**Mobile** - Bottom tab bar

| Tab | What you'll find |
|-----|------------------|
| Home | Dashboard, stats, today's workout |
| Workout | Weekly plan, all exercises |
| Diet | Macros, meals, water, supplements |
| Progress | Weight logs, chart, BMI, muscle map |
| Streak | Calendar, streaks, achievements |
| Profile | Your info and settings |

## Data Storage

All your data saves automatically in your browser. Close and reopen - everything is still there. Use the Reset button in Profile to start over.

## Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection for images and AI Coach
- Anthropic API key for AI Coach (optional)

## Tips

- Log weight daily for accurate progress charts
- Complete all exercises on workout days for bonus XP
- Use the water tracker to stay hydrated
- Check achievements for extra motivation
- Edit your profile anytime if goals change
